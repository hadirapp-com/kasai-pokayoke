Last updated: January 2026

This Application is an internal application developed and used exclusively by employees and authorized personnel within the factory environment. It is not intended for public use.

The Application may collect the following types of information for operational and quality control purposes:

Collected information is used solely to:

4. Data Sharing

The Application does not share personal or operational data with third parties. All data is accessed only by authorized internal systems and personnel.

5. Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are implemented to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to support operational, compliance, and audit requirements within the factory environment.

7. User Rights

As this is an internal application, access, correction, or deletion of data is managed in accordance with company policies and internal procedures.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in internal processes or regulatory requirements. Updates will be communicated internally when applicable.

9. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the Application, please contact the internal IT or system administrator.
